A little about us

Our Story

Over a decade of cooking for Quadra Village.

The beginning

Part & Parcel

Nestled in the heart of Quadra Village, Part & Parcel has been a gathering place for over a decade. What started as a simple idea — great food, warm service, an honest glass of wine — has grown into one of Victoria's most beloved neighbourhood restaurants.

Our name says it all. We believe the best meals are made of many parts — seasonal ingredients, thoughtful preparation, genuine hospitality — and that each one is parcel of a bigger experience. A meal here isn't just dinner. It's the conversation, the wine, the warmth, the neighbourhood.

From our very first service to today, our goal has stayed the same: to be the kind of place where you feel at home the moment you walk through the door.

Our philosophy
"We cook with the seasons. Our kitchen draws from the incredible bounty of Vancouver Island, building plates that are vibrant, considered, and meant to be shared."
— The Part & Parcel Kitchen
What drives us

Seasonal, Local, Shared

Every season brings new inspiration to our kitchen. Spring garlic and foraged greens give way to summer stone fruit, then root vegetables and braising cuts in the cooler months. Our menu is a living document — always changing, always in tune with what's growing around us.

Behind the bar, we pour low intervention wines from small producers who care as much about their craft as we do about ours. Our cocktail programme is creative, seasonal, and built on the same philosophy: quality ingredients, honest techniques, no shortcuts.

And through it all, our team brings warm, kind, and knowledgeable service. Because the best part of any meal is how it makes you feel.

Our neighbourhood

Quadra Village

Quadra Village is one of Victoria's most charming pockets — a mix of independent shops, creative studios, and community gathering spots. We're proud to be part of this neighbourhood and to cook for the people who make it what it is.

Whether you live around the corner or you're visiting Victoria for the first time, we hope Part & Parcel feels like your spot. Pull up a chair. You're welcome here.

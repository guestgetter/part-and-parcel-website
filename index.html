Neighborhood to table

The Quadra Village Journey

Part & Parcel starts long before service. Every day begins in the neighbourhood, moves through our kitchen, and lands at your table as seasonal shared plates. This is how our world comes together.

Explore Our Story

The Block

We cook in the heart of Quadra Village, shaped by independent makers, artists, and neighbours who give this corner of Victoria its spirit.

The Kitchen

Our team builds the menu around what is fresh and in season, so each service reflects what Vancouver Island is offering right now.

The Plate

Small plates are designed to be passed, tasted, and layered. Pair with low intervention wines and cocktails made with the same seasonal mindset.

The Table

From quick lunch to date night to Feast With Us, every experience is built around warm hospitality and food that brings people together.

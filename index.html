Since 2013

A decade of cooking for our neighbourhood

What started as a simple idea — great food, warm service, an honest glass of wine — has grown into one of Victoria's most beloved restaurants. We opened our doors in Quadra Village with a mission to serve seasonal, thoughtful food without the pretense.

Our Philosophy

Simple, Seasonal, Sincere.

We believe in letting ingredients speak for themselves. Our menu changes with the seasons, highlighting the best produce Vancouver Island has to offer. From our house-made pastas to our creative vegetable-forward plates, every dish tells a story of local abundance.
